Starting a Garden

March 7, 2021

It all started with one pack of seeds...

I went to my favorite corner store, Cole Hardware, to pick up a screwdriver one day. I left with far more than just your everyday Phillips head, however. I picked up my first packet of seeds, just for fun. Nothing wrong with adding some sugar snap peas to your life!

Since that fateful day, I’ve been back to the store too many times to count and I can really feel my green thumb starting to develop. I now own a proper shovel, a watering can (shaped like an elephant), and a bunch of colored pots. I am realizing how much I like having greenery around me. I am now up to 4 outdoor plants and 3 indoor plants, and I can assure you that these numbers will only continue to grow.

In my outdoor vegetable garden, I am growing:
• sugar snap peas
• carrots
• squash

Stay tuned for garden updates. Heck, maybe I’ll start my own gardening blog now that I know how!
